  • Installation of outdoor air temperature sensors 150 €

The devices differ in configuration and the presence of subsystems, depending on this they can simultaneously perform different functions to prevent accidents on the railway

Main functions of the equipment:

  • Temperature control of axle boxes of wagons and locomotive units. To detect wheel bearing failure.
  • Temperature control of the undercarriage part of the wheels of wagons and locomotive units. To identify jammed wheel brake pads.
  • Control of dragging parts of rolling stock.
  • Control of wheel tread surface deformation.
  • Rail temperature control. To prevent the widening of the railway track.
